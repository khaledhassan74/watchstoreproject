How to choose right watch for you.

A common display for watches, Analogue watches display time via hands on the dial and include the hour hand, the minute hand, and the second hand. These watches are more traditional and elegant compared to a digital watch. These timepieces offer varied designs and are highly durable, making them a great choice for all occasions.

Quartz: This is the common type of mechanism used in watches and is known for its accuracy and durability. Compared to mechanical watches, quartz watches are more affordable & practical and can be distinguished from the ‘jump’ the second hand makes, when moving from one second to another. Mechanical: These watches have a rotor attached to the movement; run by mechanical parts. They can be either hand-wound (self–winding) or automatic (the rotor spins as your wrist moves). The motion of the second hand is smooth & sweeping compared to quartz watches. However, mechanical watches are more expensive and not as accurate as quartz watches.
